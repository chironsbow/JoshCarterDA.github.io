A Story of Heritage, Passion, and Purpose

Born to Liberian immigrants, I am a proud first-generation Americanâ€”a blend of resilient heritage and boundless opportunity. My family's journey from New Jersey to Georgia in the mid-1990s laid the foundation of my life's narrative: embracing change, valuing diversity, and pursuing growth. At the University of Georgia, my fascination with the human mind led me to a degree in Cognitive Neuroscience. This field not only quenched my thirst for understanding human behavior but also sharpened my analytical skills, a cornerstone of my professional journey. For over a decade, I navigated the vibrant world of television, working at a station renowned for selling high-end jewelry. This experience wasn't just about sales; it was a masterclass in the art of communication, understanding customer needs, and building enduring relationships. Parallel to my career, my love for languages flourished. I dedicated eight years to studying Spanish, a journey still ripe with the promise of future travels to Spanish-speaking countries. Most of my professional life has been anchored in sales, where I've honed my ability to connect, persuade, and lead. Now, as I pivot towards a data analyst role, I bring with me a rich tapestry of experiences and a commitment to harness data for insightful, impactful decisions. Beyond my career, my heart beats for a cause close to my heritage. As a board member of a charity founded by my family, I am deeply involved in supporting the educational needs of children in West Africa. This work is not just a responsibility; it's a reflection of my identity and values. As I step into the world of data analysis, I carry with me a blend of cognitive insight, sales expertise, and a commitment to make a difference. Friendly, focused, and ever-charismatic, I am ready to embrace new challenges and contribute meaningfully to this dynamic field.
